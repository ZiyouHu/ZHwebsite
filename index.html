Contextualization by Transformers

Assisted in conducting experiments comparing CANINE (character tokenization) and BERT (subword tokenization) in transformers for contextualizing word embeddings. Despite the slightly lower performance, CANINE proved more flexible in word embeddings than BERT.



Modeling NYC Rat's Activities

Developed a Poisson model to analyze rat activities during New York City's rodent inspections spanning 2010 to 2014. Attained probability of encountering rat activities, influenced by the total number of inspections, overshadowing the impact of seasonal variations with model accountability of 76% of explained variation.
